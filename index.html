History of Team Fortress

Team Fortress started as a mod for Quake in 1996, introducing class-based gameplay that quickly gained popularity. Valve acquired the rights and released **Team Fortress Classic** in 1999, setting the stage for the masterpiece that is Team Fortress 2.

TF2 was officially released in 2007 as part of *The Orange Box*, instantly captivating players with its unique art style, humor, and gameplay. Over the years, it has grown into a cultural phenomenon, with regular updates, community content, and an enduring legacy.
